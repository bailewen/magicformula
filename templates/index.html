How it works

Magic Formula Metrics

  • EY (Earnings Yield) = EBIT / Enterprise Value
  • ROC (Return on Capital) = EBIT / (Net Working Capital + Net Fixed Assets)
  • MF Score = Combined rank (lower is better)
